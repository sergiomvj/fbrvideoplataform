## Story

**As a** system owner,  
**I want** review, composition and render lifecycles persisted with full traceability,  
**so that** decisions, operator actions and external render progress are never lost.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Review queue items and review actions are persisted in PostgreSQL.
2. Asset scores, score breakdowns and operational decisions are persisted.
3. Compositions and composition slots are persisted.
4. Render jobs and render job events are persisted.
5. Render and review routes no longer depend on ephemeral in-memory stores.
6. Full traceability exists from scene brief -> candidate -> score -> review/decision -> composition -> render job.
