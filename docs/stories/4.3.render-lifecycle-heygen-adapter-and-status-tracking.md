## Story

**As a** render orchestration subsystem,  
**I want** to convert internal compositions into HeyGen-compatible execution jobs and track their lifecycle,  
**so that** productions can be rendered externally while the system remains authoritative about status and outcomes.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Internal compositions from both templates can be transformed into a HeyGen execution payload through an adapter.
2. Render jobs are persisted with lifecycle states and external identifiers.
3. Status progression from queued to completed/failed is tracked and queryable.
4. External integration failures are isolated and do not corrupt upstream composition state.
5. The resulting render state is consumable by the UI and operational notifications.
