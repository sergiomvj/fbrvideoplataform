## Story

**As a** production operator,  
**I want** the production detail contract and preview surface to match the promised operational scope,  
**so that** I can trust the detail screen as the canonical inspection surface before and during render.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Backend and frontend agree on the exact detail payload required for Story `5.4`.
2. The production-detail API returns all fields that the agreed operational contract requires.
3. The detail screen renders that contract without placeholder gaps or hidden missing sections.
4. If narrative/brief/composition context is in scope for `5.4`, it is exposed and rendered explicitly; if not, the story is narrowed/documented to the true scope.
5. Story `5.4` becomes re-reviewable as compliant.
