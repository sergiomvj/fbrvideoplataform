## Story

**As a** pipeline owner,  
**I want** Story 2.2 and Story 2.3 to operate on persisted, authoritative narrative artifacts,  
**so that** structuring and visual planning form a deterministic chain instead of recomputing transient outputs.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The structuring step persists an authoritative narrative artifact linked to the production aggregate.
2. The visual-planning step consumes the persisted narrative artifact rather than rerunning structuring from raw production input.
3. Narrative blocks/scenes preserve stable identifiers that remain traceable into visual briefs.
4. Failure paths distinguish structuring failure from visual-planning failure without collapsing them into the same transient recomputation path.
5. Stories 2.2 and 2.3 can be re-reviewed as compliant against their original contracts.
