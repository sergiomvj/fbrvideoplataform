## Story

**As a** system owner,  
**I want** a complete and explicit submission flow from internal composition to external AI video APIs,  
**so that** render execution happens through a reliable, auditable and provider-ready pipeline.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The system defines a canonical internal submission contract for external AI video providers.
2. The render submission flow explicitly covers: pre-submit validation, payload generation, provider submission, external job registration, polling/webhook reconciliation and terminal status handling.
3. Provider-specific payload mapping is isolated in adapters and not spread through routes.
4. Submission failures, validation failures and provider failures are distinguishable and persisted.
5. The flow is documented clearly enough to support additional specialized video providers beyond the initial one.
6. Render lifecycle APIs expose a stable backend-authoritative contract for frontend and orchestration usage.
