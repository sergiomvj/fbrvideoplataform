## Story

**As a** production operator,  
**I want** a workspace for media curation and human review,  
**so that** I can quickly inspect, approve, reject or requery plausible candidates with full contextual visibility.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The workspace shows scene list, candidate assets, scores and rationales in a structured layout.
2. Review-queue items are retrievable and actionable from the UI.
3. Operators can approve, reject, substitute or requery candidates through the secured gateway.
4. Manual-fixed assets and auto-approved assets are visually distinguished.
5. The interface makes it obvious why a candidate is in review.
