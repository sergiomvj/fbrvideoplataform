## Story

**As a** composition subsystem,  
**I want** to assemble `Presenter Short` productions into a structured internal timeline,  
**so that** render execution can happen from a stable, template-faithful composition contract.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The system can build an internal composition for `Presenter Short` productions from structured narrative + approved assets/bindings.
2. The composition respects the closed-template constraints of Presenter Short.
3. Variation selection influences composition in controlled ways without changing template identity.
4. Manual asset bindings override automatic selections when present.
5. The output is render-ready internally but still provider-agnostic.
