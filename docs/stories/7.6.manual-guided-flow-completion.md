## Story

**As an** editorial operator,  
**I want** a complete manual guided flow with pre-selected assets and binding rules,  
**so that** I can produce sensitive or bespoke videos without relying on automatic sourcing.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The manual pre-flow contract is explicit and documented.
2. The backend exposes APIs to create, list and remove manual bindings.
3. Manual mode in the wizard has a distinct pre-flow experience.
4. Operators can declare required, fixed, preferred and prohibited assets when applicable.
5. Composition consumes persisted manual bindings without violating template rules.
