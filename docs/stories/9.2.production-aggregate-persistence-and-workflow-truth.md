## Story

**As a** platform owner,  
**I want** productions, inputs and workflow transitions persisted in PostgreSQL,  
**so that** the system survives restarts and reflects a single authoritative workflow truth.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Productions are persisted in PostgreSQL.
2. Production inputs are persisted as normalized snapshots.
3. Workflow state transitions are persisted append-only.
4. The system no longer depends on `_productions_store`.
5. Aggregate transition rules remain enforced in the domain layer after persistence is introduced.
6. Production list and detail endpoints are backed by persisted state.
