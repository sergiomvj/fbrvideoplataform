## Story

**As a** platform operator,  
**I want** structured auditing, tracing and operational telemetry built into the platform early,  
**so that** every later workflow can be diagnosed, reviewed and governed without retrofitting observability.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Structured logging exists with identifiers for production, scene, asset, render job and user/organization context where applicable.
2. Audit events exist for key operational decisions and state changes.
3. Baseline metrics exist for pipeline stage timing, decision outcomes and integration failures.
4. A minimal operational dashboard or documented metric map exists for later monitoring setup.
5. The implementation is reusable by all subsequent stories without introducing duplicate telemetry patterns.
