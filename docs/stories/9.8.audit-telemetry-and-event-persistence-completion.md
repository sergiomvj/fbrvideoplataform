## Story

**As a** platform operator,  
**I want** audit, telemetry and domain events persisted in a reusable way,  
**so that** key operational actions remain diagnosable, governable and historically traceable.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Key audit events are persisted, not just emitted to logs.
2. Domain events relevant to production, review and render are recorded with traceable identifiers.
3. The audit service remains reusable by downstream stories.
4. Event persistence includes actor, entity, before/after or equivalent actionable metadata where relevant.
5. Existing structured logging and metrics continue to work alongside persisted audit/event records.
6. The resulting implementation satisfies the intent of story `1.4` fully.
