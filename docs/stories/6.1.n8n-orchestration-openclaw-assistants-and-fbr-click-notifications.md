## Story

**As a** system owner,  
**I want** orchestration, assistant hooks and human-facing notifications integrated into the runtime,  
**so that** the platform can operate in the FBR ecosystem with controlled automation and clear human escalation.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Core workflow events can be consumed by n8n for orchestration purposes.
2. The system exposes the necessary hooks/contracts for OpenClaw-based assistant behaviors where appropriate.
3. Relevant human events are mappable to FBR-Click notifications.
4. Orchestration remains outside the domain core and does not duplicate domain logic.
5. Failure and escalation paths are represented clearly in the orchestration layer.
