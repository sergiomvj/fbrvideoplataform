## Story

**As a** platform operator,  
**I want** Story 8.1 completed with real persistence and runtime resolution,  
**so that** provider enablement, disablement and sourcing behavior survive restarts and can be governed operationally.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Provider records are persisted in PostgreSQL and survive restart.
2. CRUD and enable/disable operations use persistence as the source of truth rather than only an in-memory registry.
3. The media sourcing layer resolves active providers from persisted configuration.
4. Provider-level traceability appears in sourcing and integration logs with stable provider identity.
5. Story 8.1 can be re-reviewed as compliant against its original acceptance criteria.
