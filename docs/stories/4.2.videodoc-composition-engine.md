## Story

**As a** composition subsystem,  
**I want** to assemble `VideoDoc Narrated` productions into a structured internal timeline,  
**so that** richer multi-asset productions can be rendered from a stable and auditable composition model.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The system can build an internal composition for `VideoDoc Narrated` productions using structured narrative and approved assets.
2. The composition respects VideoDoc template constraints and sequence logic.
3. Variation selection influences composition in controlled, template-safe ways.
4. Manual bindings override automatic selections when present.
5. The output remains provider-agnostic and ready for render adaptation.
