## Story

**As a** backlog owner,  
**I want** the story ledger and status metadata cleaned up,  
**so that** distribution, QA and remediation planning use one canonical and trustworthy backlog.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Every reviewed story has one unambiguous status value with no contradictory header/body metadata.
2. Stories marked implemented are backed by real implementation evidence, or they are downgraded to the correct status.
3. Duplicate or confusing artifacts such as parallel migration-plan references are reconciled into one canonical path.
4. Backlog consumers can determine implementation state, review state and readiness without interpretation.
5. The affected `7.x` and `8.x` stories are safe to use for assignment after this cleanup.
